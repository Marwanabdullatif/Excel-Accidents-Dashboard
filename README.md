# 🚗 Road Accidents & Casualties Executive Dashboard (Advanced Excel)

An interactive, single-page executive dashboard developed in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze over **100,000+ records** of road accident data. This project transforms raw operational data into actionable business intelligence, highlighting critical safety KPIs and high-risk factors to support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Key Features
* **Tool:** Microsoft Excel 📈
* **Data Transformation:** Data cleaning, formatting, and structural preparation.
* **Analysis Engine:** Advanced formulas (`XLOOKUP`, `Nested IFs`) and **Pivot Tables** for data aggregation.
* **Reporting Layout:** Interactive **Slicers** (Weather, Year, Urban/Rural, Light Conditions) for seamless, dynamic filtering.

---

## 📊 Key Insights & Metrics Captured
Based on the comprehensive data analysis conducted across multiple dimensions:
* **Total Accidents:** 106,656 recorded incidents.
* **Total Casualties:** 417,882 individuals affected.
* **Total Vehicles Involved:** 563,301 vehicles.
* **Speed Limits:** Outlined correlation between higher speed limits (Average 39 km/H) and accident severity.
* **Peak Year:** Identified 2021 as the highest year for accidents compared to 2022.

---

## 🚀 Analytical Deep-Dive (Pivot Analysis)
The data pipeline handles granular cross-tabulations including:
1. **Environmental Impacts:** Mapping accident frequency against weather conditions (Rain, Fog, Snow) and light status (Daylight vs. Darkness).
2. **Geographical Distribution:** Comparing urban density traffic vs. rural road casualties.
3. **Infrastructure Factors:** Aggregating data based on junction control systems (Traffic signals, Stop signs, Uncontrolled intersections).
